A few things while it talks:

Nudge the speed until it matches the pace of your thoughts. Turn on auto-advance and it flows straight into the next chapter, no taps required. And lock your phone — the voice keeps reading, with controls right on your lock screen.

Reading and listening always stay in sync: switch between them anytime and you're exactly where you left off.
