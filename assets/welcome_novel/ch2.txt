Time to get you a real story.

Head back to your Library and tap the "+" button at the top. There are three ways in:

<img src="https://cdn.arcreaders.app/welcome_novel/img/add_three_ways.png">

Search — know a title, or half-remember one? Type it in and ArcReader looks across many reading sources at once.

Browse — choose "Pick a Source" and wander through reading sites like a bookstore, by genre or popularity.

Paste a link — copy a novel's link from your browser and paste it in. ArcReader figures out the rest: title, cover, chapters. You can even share a page straight from your browser to ArcReader.

Whichever you pick, you'll see a preview — cover, synopsis, chapter count — before anything lands in your library.

Go on, try it now if you like. This chapter will wait.
